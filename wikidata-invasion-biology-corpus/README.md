The two CSV files in this repository, `Hypotheses.csv` and `Publications.csv`, are extracted from a larger dataset compiled as part of Wikidata's [WikiProject Invasion Biology](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:WikiProject_Invasion_biology) as of July 2024 and published on Zenodo. These files specifically include hypotheses and corresponding publication DOIs that guided the search scope for publications via the ASK service.

For access to the complete original dataset, please visit the DOI [10.5281/zenodo.12518036](https://www.doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.12518036).

**Authors**: Mietchen, D., Jeschke, J. M., Bernard-Verdier, M., Heger, T., Musseau, C., & Tyszka, S. (2024). Invasion Biology Corpus July 2024 [Data set]. Zenodo. [https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.12518037](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.12518037).
